Succession
Planning

Your legacy is not an afterthought. It is the ultimate destination of every financial decision you make, and SELEQT helps you design it with precision.

Wealth created over a lifetime can be lost or fragmented within a generation without the right structures in place. SELEQT's succession planning practice ensures your wealth transfers smoothly, efficiently, and exactly as you intend.

In India, succession is governed by a complex web of personal laws, inheritance statutes, and tax regulations, made even more intricate for those with assets or heirs across multiple jurisdictions. SELEQT brings together legal, tax, and financial expertise to design structures that are both legally robust and emotionally considered.

Whether you are a first-generation wealth creator, a promoter planning a business transition, or a second-generation heir seeking to professionalise family wealth governance, our succession advisory offers a structured path forward.

Private Family Trusts

Setting up and administering irrevocable or revocable private trusts, protecting assets from creditors, disputes, and fragmented inheritance while enabling controlled distribution to beneficiaries.

Will Drafting & Estate Planning

Professionally drafted wills that align with your intentions and withstand legal challenges, covering Indian and overseas assets, digital assets, and business interests.

Business Succession

Structuring the transition of a family business, including shareholding restructuring, buy-sell agreements, ESOP frameworks, and next-generation leadership planning.

Cross-Border Estate Planning

Managing assets and beneficiaries across India and multiple countries, leveraging international trusts, holding structures, and DTAA provisions.

Family Governance

Establishing Family Charters, Investment Policy Statements, Family Councils, and governance frameworks to maintain unity and direction across generations.

Philanthropic Structuring

Setting up charitable trusts, foundations, and donor-advised structures for families looking to embed philanthropy into their legacy.

A Private Family Trust, in particular, has emerged as the premier structure for Indian HNI families, offering asset protection, tax efficiency, and generational continuity in a single framework. SELEQT guides you through every step: from structuring and registration to ongoing trustee advisory and beneficiary coordination.

The conversation about succession is never easy. But it is always necessary. SELEQT creates a safe, structured space to have it, and translates your wishes into durable legal and financial architecture.

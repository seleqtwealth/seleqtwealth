# SELEQT SEO Playbook

A working checklist for improving search rankings and organic traffic.

## This week (do once)

- [ ] **Set up Google Business Profile** at [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
  - Add SELEQT, address, phone, website, business hours
  - Category: "Financial planner" or "Investment service"
  - Add 5–10 photos (office, team, branding)
  - Verify via postcard (~7 days)
- [ ] **Lock the Web3Forms key to seleqtwealth.in** in the [web3forms.com](https://web3forms.com) dashboard
- [ ] **Submit site to Bing** at [bing.com/webmasters](https://bing.com/webmasters) (same sitemap.xml). Covers Bing + DuckDuckGo + ChatGPT search
- [ ] **List on free Indian directories**: Justdial, Sulekha, IndiaMart, Google Maps, Yellow Pages India
- [ ] **Create LinkedIn company page** for SELEQT with the website URL

## This month (one-time technical wins)

- [ ] Add **FAQ schema** to each service page (enables "People also ask" features)
- [ ] Add **Service schema** to each service page (Investments, Tax, Insurance, Succession)
- [ ] Add **LocalBusiness schema** alongside FinancialService (Maps eligibility)
- [ ] Add **breadcrumb schema** to sub-pages
- [ ] **Self-host Google Fonts** (faster page load)
- [ ] Create a **1200×630 og-image.png** for rich link previews
- [ ] Run [PageSpeed Insights](https://pagespeed.web.dev/) and fix anything red
- [ ] Add **conversion tracking** in GA4 for contact form submissions
- [ ] Add **alt text** to all images (none yet, but as you add them)

## Content strategy — the real game

**Angle:** NRI + cross-border. Don't fight generic "wealth management" keywords; target long-tail queries where you can win.

- [ ] Build an `/insights.html` section + individual article pages
- [ ] Publish 1–2 articles per month, 1,500–2,500 words each
- [ ] Each article needs: clear long-tail keyword in the title, H2s with related keywords, 1,500+ words, real expertise, 2–3 internal links to service pages, author bio

## Backlinks — slow, high-trust

- [ ] Pitch one guest article per quarter to: Mint, ET Wealth, MoneyControl, LiveMint, Business Standard
- [ ] Get listed in: AMFI distributor directory, ICAI member listings, IRDAI broker listings
- [ ] NRI publications: Khaleej Times (Dubai), Eastern Eye (UK), India Abroad (US)
- [ ] Local Ahmedabad: Gujarat Chamber of Commerce, Ahmedabad Management Association, CA/CS networks
- [ ] Speak at events / podcasts (most include a website link in writeups)

## Weekly habits (5 min, every Monday)

- [ ] Search Console → **Performance**: note queries at positions 11–20 (small tweaks push them to page 1)
- [ ] Search Console → **Pages**: fix any "Discovered but not indexed" pages (usually thin content)
- [ ] GA4 → **Realtime**: confirm tracking is working
- [ ] GA4 → **Acquisition → Traffic source**: see which channels bring visitors

## Monthly habits

- [ ] Publish 1–2 new Insights articles
- [ ] Update Google Business Profile with a post/photo
- [ ] Search Console → **Enhancements**: fix any structured data errors
- [ ] Refresh 1 older article with new info (Google rewards updated content)
- [ ] Note top 5 ranking keywords + position; track over time

## What NOT to do (waste of money)

- Buying backlinks from cheap services (penalty risk)
- Keyword stuffing (ignored by Google)
- Generic SEO agency packages under ₹30K/month
- Targeting generic head terms like "wealth management" — can't outrank ICICI/HDFC/Kotak
- Thin pages under 500 words — Google deindexes them

## Realistic expectations

| When | What |
|---|---|
| Month 1–2 | Almost nothing. Google is still discovering you. Don't panic. |
| Month 3–4 | First branded searches in Console. ~50–200 visits/month. |
| Month 6 | With 6–10 articles published: 500–1,500 visits/month, ranking for several long-tail queries. |
| Month 12 | With consistent content + backlinks: 3,000–10,000 visits/month, meaningful lead flow. |

For a wealth firm where one client = ₹5 lakh+/year, you only need a handful of qualified leads/year for SEO to pay back many times over.

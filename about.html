About SELEQT

Wealth is rarely built in a single generation, and neither is trust.

SELEQT was built for people who want their wealth handled with care and continuity, by one team that knows the whole picture, rather than scattered across a dozen intermediaries who each see only a slice.

We are a boutique firm by choice, working with a deliberately small number of families so that each relationship is handled by people who actually know your full financial picture.

SELEQT is a private wealth advisory firm serving Indian and global clients through an integrated approach to investing, structuring, protection, and legacy planning.

We work with entrepreneurs, professionals, NRIs, and multi-generational families whose financial lives now run across businesses, countries, and generations, and help them keep the whole thing coherent instead of scattered.

Our approach is deliberately relationship-led. You work with one advisory relationship that knows your situation, not a rotating set of intermediaries who each see only a slice of it.

We started SELEQT on a simple conviction: that complex wealth deserves serious, long-term advice, handled by people you can trust with discretion.

What we stand for

i.

Relationship-led

Built around long-term relationships and a real understanding of each client's full financial picture, not one-off transactions.

ii.

One team, one balance sheet

Investments, tax, insurance, and succession are not separate departments here. A single team holds the full picture.

iii.

Built for cross-border lives

FEMA, DTAA, repatriation, and global compliance are part of the brief, not an afterthought.

iv.

Deliberately small

We work with a limited number of families by design, to deliver the depth of attention private wealth requires.

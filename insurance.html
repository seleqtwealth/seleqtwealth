Insurance &
Protection Planning

Because true wealth management means protecting what you've built, and those who depend on it.

Insurance is the silent foundation of every sound financial plan. At SELEQT, we treat it as a strategic instrument, not a product to be sold, but a protection to be engineered.

Most investors are either underinsured, over-paying for coverage they don't need, or holding outdated policies that no longer align with their net worth or life stage. SELEQT conducts a comprehensive insurance audit as the first step, identifying gaps, eliminating redundancies, and optimising premiums.

We work with best-in-class insurers across life, health, property, and liability domains, and because we are advisor-led rather than agent-driven, every recommendation is product-agnostic and client-aligned. We have no incentive to recommend a more expensive policy, our only incentive is your security.

Pure Term Life Insurance

High-coverage, low-cost term plans calibrated to replace income, clear liabilities, and fund future goals in your absence.

Comprehensive Health Cover

Super top-up plans, critical illness riders, and international health cover, ensuring your family is never exposed to catastrophic medical costs.

Keyman & Business Insurance

Protecting the business against the loss of key personnel, ensuring continuity and safeguarding shareholder value.

Wealth-Building Life Plans

Select ULIPs and guaranteed savings plans used strategically for specific goals, education, retirement corpus, or liquidity-lined obligations.

NRI Insurance Solutions

Insurance planning for Non-Resident Indians, covering repatriation of claims, cross-border health cover, and Indian asset protection.

Insurance Audit & Review

A thorough review of your existing policies, identifying gaps in coverage, overlaps, and opportunities to reduce premiums without compromising protection.

For HNIs and business families, insurance also plays a crucial role in succession planning, structured life insurance can provide immediate liquidity to heirs for estate taxes and business buyouts, preventing forced asset liquidations at unfavourable times.

SELEQT advisors ensure your insurance portfolio is reviewed annually, keeping pace with life changes, new dependents, business growth, and evolving regulations.

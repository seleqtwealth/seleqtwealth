Investments

Portfolio management, alternative funds, and curated global opportunities. Research-led, concentrated, and built around your risk frame rather than a benchmark's.

At SELEQT, our investment practice combines institutional-grade research with a deeply personalised approach. We bring listed equities, alternatives, and global mandates under one strategy.

We design investment portfolios around your specific risk profile, return expectations, and investment horizon, rather than fitting you into a pre-built mould. Across listed equity, fixed income, and alternative strategies, every position is deliberate, researched, and monitored continuously by a single coordinated team.

Our offering spans discretionary Portfolio Management Services (PMS), curated access to Category I, II and III Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs), and selective access to global mandates. Whether you are building wealth, preserving it, or transitioning it across generations, we hold the full picture.

Portfolio Management Services

Discretionary equity and multi-asset portfolios for sophisticated investors, with a minimum mandate of ₹50 lakhs as per SEBI guidelines.

Alternative Investment Funds

Curated access to Category I, II and III AIFs spanning private equity, private credit, real estate, and long-short strategies.

Equity & Multi-Asset Strategies

Domestic, international, and hybrid allocations, with full transparency on positioning, risk, and performance attribution.

Customised Mandates

Each portfolio is built around your unique risk appetite, ESG preferences, sectoral exclusions, and liquidity needs.

Transparent Reporting

Detailed monthly statements, performance attribution, and real-time portfolio access through a dedicated client portal.

Dedicated Relationship Manager

A single point of contact who understands your complete financial picture and coordinates across every service line.

Our investment philosophy is grounded in conviction, not noise. We hold concentrated positions, employ deep fundamental research, and avoid the herd. Every recommendation passes through a single coordinated team that also understands your tax position, your insurance, and your succession plan, because investment decisions in isolation are rarely good investment decisions.
